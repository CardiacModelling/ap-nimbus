Privacy and Security Information

A Session cookie (a cookie in this case is a small text file which is placed on your computer by the browser) is required by this application.
Our session cookie will not hold personally identifiable information, only a session identifier to enable the web application to enforce security constraints - without which the web site will not work.

Information Commissioner's Office : Cookies Regulations and the New EU Cookie Law.

Unfortunately, as with every publicly accessible website, this application and its content and communication are at risk of attack.
